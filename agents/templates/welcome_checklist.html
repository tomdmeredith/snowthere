Planning a ski trip with kids can feel overwhelming. Trust me, I've been there - endless browser tabs, conflicting advice, and that nagging feeling you're forgetting something important.

That's why I made this checklist. It's the same one I use for my own family trips.

2-4 Weeks Before

☐ Book ski lessons (book early - they fill up!)
☐ Reserve equipment rentals
☐ Purchase lift tickets or passes
☐ Book lodging (ski-in/ski-out = sanity saver)
☐ Arrange airport transfers if flying
☐ Check passport expiration (6+ months!)

1 Week Before

☐ Check weather forecast
☐ Download resort trail map
☐ Confirm all reservations
☐ Pack (see packing list below!)
☐ Charge devices, download offline entertainment

Packing Essentials (Per Person)

☐ Ski jacket & pants (waterproof!)
☐ Base layers (2-3 sets)
☐ Mid layers (fleece or down)
☐ Ski socks (NOT cotton - wool or synthetic)
☐ Helmet (rent or bring)
☐ Goggles (+ spare lens for flat light)
☐ Gloves/mittens (waterproof, bring backup pair)
☐ Neck gaiter or balaclava
☐ Sunscreen SPF 50+ (altitude = intense sun)
☐ Lip balm with SPF

Kids-Specific Must-Haves

☐ Hand/toe warmers (game changer!)
☐ Snacks for chairlift (hangry kids = meltdowns)
☐ Helmet speakers for music (makes lifts fun)
☐ After-ski activities (cards, travel games)
☐ Favorite stuffed animal (you know why)

Pro tip: Print this checklist and stick it on your fridge. Check things off as you go. Future you will thank present you.
