The 5 Questions Framework

Question 1

What's your budget?

Under $5K: Consider drive-to resorts or Europe (yes, really).
$5-10K: Wide open options - US or Europe.
$10K+: Premium resorts become accessible.

Question 2

How old are your kids?

Filter for resorts rated for their age range. A "family-friendly" resort for tweens is different than one for preschoolers.

Question 3

When are you going?

Holiday weeks: Expect crowds everywhere. Book 6+ months ahead.
January: Best value, often best snow, fewer crowds.
Spring break: Hit higher elevation resorts for reliable snow.

Question 4

Is this their first trip?

If yes: Prioritize excellent ski school over big terrain. A bad first experience can turn kids off skiing forever.

Question 5

What matters most to YOU?

Convenience? Pick somewhere with easy airport access.
Charm? European villages win.
Value? Look at all-in-one packages.
Snow reliability? Check the snow history data.

Top 3 for First-Timers

Budget-conscious: Keystone, CO - Great ski school, affordable lodging
Go big: Park City, UT - Incredible terrain variety, easy airport
Adventure: Lech, Austria - Charming village, top-tier ski school, half the price of Deer Valley
